🌟 Key points

  • 🐾Emotional support animals provide comfort and stress relief to people suffering from mental health issues.
  • 🐾Dogs are the most common type of emotional support animal because they are good at gauging human emotion and take well to special training.
  • 🐾Many other types of animals also make great emotional support partners–especially for people who are allergic to or afraid of dogs.
  • 🐾An emotional support or therapy animal is not quite the same thing as a service animal, which provides physical help to its owner.

Why Choose Pet TherapyOne?

At PetTherapyOne, we are passionate about the well-being of individuals facing mental health challenges. Our pet therapy programs are designed to bring joy, comfort, and healing through the magic of pets.

Diverse Range of Support Options

We recognize the unique needs of each individual. While dogs are popular choices, we offer a diverse range of emotional support animals, including rabbits, birds, and more. Our goal is to ensure everyone can benefit from the companionship of a therapy animal.

Our Commitment to Community

PetTherapyOne is not just an agency; we are a community dedicated to making a difference. Through tailored therapy programs and active community outreach, we strive to extend the benefits of pet therapy to as many people as possible.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Discover the joy and healing power that pet therapy can bring. Whether you're seeking support for yourself or a loved one, or if you're interested in volunteering with our therapy animals, PetTherapyOne welcomes you to be a part of our compassionate community.
